Our Story

Founded with a vision to revolutionize poultry sector, SM Agro Trades Ltd has emerged as a leading supplier of premium Non-GMO soy products.

We understand the critical role that quality nutrition plays in livestock and poultry farming. That's why we've dedicated ourselves to sourcing only the finest soybean meal and agro products in India, ensuring that our cilents have access to world-class feed ingredients.

Our commitment goes beyond just supplying products – we're building partnerships that empower farmers, enhance livestock productivity, and contribute to food security.
